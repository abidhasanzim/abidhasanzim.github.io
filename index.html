I graduated with a B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from Zakir Husain College of Engineering and Technology, Aligarh Muslim University (ZHCET, AMU), India, in 2023.

My research interests broadly encompass machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and robotics. Since the summer of 2023, I have been a Research Intern at Tohoku University, working with Dr. Minoru Kuribayashi on computer vision-related problems.

I have also been fortunate to collaborate with several research groups and academic institutions during my undergraduate years, including Dr. Asad Malik and Dr. Hanzhou Wu at Monash University Malaysia; and Dr. Saleem Anwar Khan at ZHCET, AMU.

Additionally, I served as a Management Trainee for ZHCET Formula Racing, where we won the Formula Bharat 2022 Business Plan Event.
